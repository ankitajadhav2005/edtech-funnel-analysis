#  EdTech Funnel Analysis (Power BI | SQL | Python)

---

##  Business Problem
The EdTech platform is generating a high volume of leads but struggling to convert them into paying customers.

Despite consistent lead inflow, the overall conversion rate is low, leading to poor ROI and missed revenue opportunities.

Core Issue: High lead generation but low conversion due to significant drop-offs across the funnel.

---

## Objective
- Analyze the lead-to-conversion funnel  
- Identify stage-wise drop-offs  
- Understand reasons behind low conversion  
- Provide data-driven recommendations  

---

##  Dataset Overview
The dataset includes:
- Lead generation sources  
- Funnel stages (Awareness, Consideration, Conversion)  
- Customer segments  

###  Key Metrics:
- Total Leads: **360**  
- Total Conversions: **64**  
- Conversion Rate: **17.78%**

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)  
- SQL  
- Power BI  
- DAX  

---

##  Project Workflow
1. Data Cleaning using Python  
2. Data Analysis using SQL  
3. Data Modeling in Power BI  
4. Dashboard Creation  
5. Insight Generation  

---

## Funnel Analysis

### Stage-wise Drop-offs:
- Awareness: 46.11% 
- Consideration: 40.72% 
- Conversion: 44.35%

 The funnel shows consistent leakage across all stages, indicating systemic inefficiencies.

---

##  Key Insights

- Over 80% of leads are lost**, indicating low funnel efficiency  
- Highest drop-off at Awareness stage → poor lead quality or weak first interaction  
- Significant drop at Consideration stage → weak engagement and follow-ups  
- High drop at Conversion stage → pricing and trust issues  

---

##  Recommendations

- Improve targeting to attract high-intent leads  
- Strengthen follow-up strategy using multi-channel communication  
- Optimize pricing for job seekers  
- Enhance onboarding experience with demos or trials  
- Implement segment-based funnel strategies  

---

##  Expected Impact

- Estimated recovery of 30% lost leads  
- Projected **21.88% improvement in conversion rate**

 Achieved by optimizing pricing strategy and improving follow-ups before demo stage.
